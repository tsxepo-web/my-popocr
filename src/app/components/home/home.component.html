Why Use OCR and Document Understanding Models?

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and document understanding models offer significant advantages in automating and enhancing data extraction processes. OCR technology allows for the conversion of printed or handwritten text into digital format, making it easy to edit, search, and store textual information. This technology is particularly valuable in processing documents, receipts, and forms quickly and accurately.

Document understanding models, on the other hand, provide deeper insights by analyzing the context and structure of documents. They can extract and categorize data from various types of documents, such as invoices, contracts, and reports, providing structured information that is crucial for business intelligence and decision-making. These models not only improve efficiency but also reduce errors and manual workload, leading to faster and more reliable data processing.
